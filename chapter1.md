# Chapter 1

Once upon a time, there was a sheep named Woolly who lived in a small, green pasture. Woolly loved to change the color and shape of his wool. One sunny morning, he woke up feeling cheerful. The bright sun made him feel bright too! So, he decided to change his wool to a shiny golden color that sparkled in the sunlight.

As he trotted around the pasture, the other animals marveled at his dazzling look. "Wow, Woolly! You look like a sunny star!" exclaimed Benny the bunny. Woolly felt proud and danced around, showing off his golden curls. Everyone joined in dancing under the sun, making it a joyful day filled with laughter.
