# Chapter 1

Once upon a time, there was a sheep named Mr. Hooves who lived in a small, green pasture. Mr. Hooves loved to change the color and shape of his wool. One sunny morning, he woke up feeling cheerful. The bright sun made him feel bright too! So, he decided to change his wool to a shiny golden color that sparkled in the sunlight.


As he trotted around the pasture, the other animals marveled at his dazzling look. "Wow, Mr. Hooves! You look like a sunny star!" exclaimed Benny the bunny. Everyone joined in dancing under the sun, making it a joyful day filled with laughter.
