# Chapter 2

One day, dark clouds suddenly filled the sky. Mr. Hooves noticed that the weather was changing. He felt a little sad because he loved the sun. So, he decided to change his wool again. This time, he made his wool a soft, gray color, just like the clouds above.

As the rain began to fall, Mr. Hooves noticed something special. With each drop, his wool shimmered as if it held tiny diamonds. The other animals loved his new look. "You look so cozy, Mr. Hooves!" said Lila the lamb. Together, they found joy in the rain, jumping in puddles and enjoying the fresh smell of the earth.
