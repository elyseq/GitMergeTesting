# Chapter 3

A few days later, a strong wind blew through the pasture. Woolly felt excited and adventurous. He transformed his wool into light and fluffy shapes that danced just like the sky.

Feeling playful, Woolly decided to run across the field, letting the wind guide him. The animals cheered, trying to keep up with his spinning wool shapes. "You’re like a cloud that dances, Woolly!" shouted Benny. They all ran and played, feeling happy in the windy weather.
