# Chapter 4

Finally, one evening, the sun set and stars twinkled in the dark sky. Mr. Hooves felt calm and dreamy. He changed his wool to a deep, dark blue sprinkled with tiny white lights resembling stars.

As he lay down, the other animals gathered around him, gazing at his beautiful wool. "You look like the night sky, Mr. Hooves," whispered Lila. They shared stories under the starry sky until they fell asleep, dreaming of new adventures.

Mr. Hooves knew that no matter the weather, he could always find joy in changing. And so, he continued to change with the world around him, a colorful sheep in a vast universe.
